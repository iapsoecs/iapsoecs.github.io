We are seeking a Senior Lecturer in Physical Oceanography to join our team at the Institute for Marine and Antarctic Studies (IMAS), University of Tasmania (https://www.utas.edu.au/imas)

This position will provide high‑quality research and scientific leadership in physical oceanography. Its core purpose is to strengthen the links between our world-leading blue‑water and climate research and the variability of near‑shore physical and biogeochemical processes across Australia’s marine territory.

The position description and more details are available on the UTAS Careers website - https://careers.utas.edu.au/en/job/502141/senior-lecturer-in-physical-oceanography

Senior Lecturer in Physical Oceanography

- Full time, fixed term to August 2028 (2.5 years)
- Balanced research-teaching load
- Academic Level C (base salary within the range of AUD $138,536 to $157,330 plus 17% superannuation)
- Applications close 15th February 2026
- International applicants are welcome to apply
